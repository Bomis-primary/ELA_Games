Homophones (Sound Alike)

These words sound exactly the same when spoken, but they are written with different spellings and mean different things!

  • 👂 hear (using your ears) vs. 📍 here (in this spot)
  • 🌾 flour (baking ingredient) vs. 🌸 flower (blooming plant)
  • 📖 tale (imaginary story) vs. 🐕 tail (part of an animal's body)
🎯

Homonyms (Spelled Alike)

These words are spelled and sounded identically, but their definitions shift completely based on the sentence.

  • 🦇 bat (a little flying animal) vs. ⚾ bat (wooden sports stick)
  • 🌲 bark (exterior layer of a tree) vs. 🐕 bark (sound made by dogs)
  • ⌚ watch (wrist clock) vs. 👀 watch (to view with attention)
