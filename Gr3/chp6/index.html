Marathon Run

Subject & Predicate Challenge

🏃‍♂️ How to Play: Guide your marathon runner down the stadium tracks by changing lanes!

🔥 Marathon Rules:

  • Read the sentence and prompt displayed at the top of the stadium monitor.
  • Steer your runner into the lane with the correct phrase gate!
  • Pass the gate successfully to earn a massive speed boost, collect water bottles, and pull ahead of your rival!
  • Crashing into the wrong gate will cause your runner to trip, slowing you down while your rival dashes forward!

🎮 Controls: Tap the Lane Buttons below, tap lanes directly on the screen, swipe left/right, or use Keyboard Left/Right Arrow keys!
