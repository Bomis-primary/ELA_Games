Blackboard Lesson

Letters we write, but do not say out loud!

🤫 Silent L

Often quiet after A, O, or U.

• walk (sounds like wok)
• half

🤫 Silent H

Quiet at the start or after G/R.

• hour (sounds like our)
• ghost

🤫 Silent T

Often quiet in -sten, -tle, -tch.

• listen
• castle

🤫 Silent G

Quiet when next to N.

• sign
• gnome
