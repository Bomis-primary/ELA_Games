Adjective Scale Quest

Degrees of Comparison (Grade 3)

🎯 Objective: Read each sentence carefully and identify the correct degree of comparison!

📊 Three Degrees of Adjectives:

  • Positive Degree: Describes 1 noun without comparing (e.g., fast, bright, happy).
  • Comparative Degree: Compares 2 nouns (adds -er, -ier, or more) (e.g., faster, happier, more helpful).
  • Superlative Degree: Compares 3+ nouns (adds -est, -iest, or most) (e.g., fastest, happiest, most helpful).

🎮 Controls: Tap or click the launching pod on the interactive arena that correctly completes the sentence!
